Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 slice of bread
  • ½ ripe avocado
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  • Toast your slice of bread until golden and firm.
  • Remove the pit from your avocado. Use a big spoon to scoop out the flesh. Put it in a bowl and mash it up with a fork until it’s as smooth as you like it. Mix in a pinch of salt
  • Spread avocado on top of your toast. Enjoy as-is or top with any extras
